## Overview

RD63 "Vespa" is a 3.5" cinewhoop built on the SpeedyBee Bee35 platform with HDZero digital video. Designed for smooth indoor/proximity flying with HD recording.

**Repository:** `spydmobile/RD63_Vespa`
**Location:** `projects/drone_management/drones/RD63_Vespa/`
**Platform:** 3.5" Cinewhoop (SpeedyBee Bee35)
